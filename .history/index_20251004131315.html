Why Go with us?

We have thirty years of experience in conveyancing and property related legal matters. As a small firm centrally located in Pretoria, we have experienced staff that have been with us for over twenty years. A vast number of conveyancing transactions have been professionally executed for clients, parastatals, banks and private individuals over the past three decades. Our firm also specializes in Sectional Title matters as well as Retirement Developments. We have wide ranging experience in respect of the Sectional Titles Act as well as the Sectional Titles Schemes Management Act. Our firm also provides specialist advice on the Development Schemes for Retired Persons Act as we act for a wide range of retirement villages and have specialist expertise in the field of ownership and life right arrangements in such developments.
